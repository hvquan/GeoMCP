Step-by-Step Geometry Drawer

Enter each construction step on a separate line, then click "Draw from steps" to build the figure.

Rule-based parser (no LLM): supports circles (center + optional radius), points on a circle, lines / segments / rays, parallel and perpendicular, midpoints, symmetry, special triangles, perpendicular bisector / angle bisector / altitude / median, circumscribed and inscribed circles, and basic quadrilaterals (square, rectangle, rhombus, parallelogram, trapezoid, isosceles trapezoid). You can drag source points to interact — the system recomputes dependent objects from the step chain.
