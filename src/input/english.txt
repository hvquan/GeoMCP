Given a circle with center O and diameter CD. Draw a tangent Cx to the circle through C. Let E be a point on the circle with center O. Draw a line through O perpendicular to the segment CE and intersecting the tangent Cx at A.
a) Prove that AE is a tangent to the circle with center O.
b) Draw a tangent to the circle O at D. This tangent intersects AE at B. Prove that AC + BD = AB and that triangle AOB is right-angled at vertex O.
c) Draw EH perpendicular to CD at H. Prove that lines AD and BC intersect at the midpoint of EH.
